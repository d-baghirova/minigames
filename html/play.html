Piano Simulator

Turn on the sound and start playing with the Piano Simulator! You can play using mouse or using key-board!

White piano keys of first octave can be accessed by clicking on q, w, e, r, t, y, u and black piano keys of first octave can be accessed by clicking on 2, 3, 5, 6, 7. White piano keys of second octave can be accessed by clicking on z, x, v, b, n, m, , and black piano keys of first octave can be accessed by clicking on s, d, g, h, j.

The are some nice melodies that you can play on two octaves, for instance first part of The Royal Ballet.
