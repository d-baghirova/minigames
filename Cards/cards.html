Cards Memory Game

Try to memorize what's on the cards rhen click Play to start the game. Click on a card to see its image, if you will click on a random place random you'll see random card's image. After you clicked on two different cards they will be compared and if they are similar they will stay like this, in other cases their image will be hidded again. You have limited ammount of clickes to win the game. If you manage to see all cards' images before the Play game menu appears you will win!

Games are available only for computers with minimum screen width 1100px.
